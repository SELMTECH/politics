Chief Tosan Atle Aiboni

A legal luminary and devoted LUCOSA leader

Chief Tosan Atle Aiboni is a distinguished legal professional whose journey through corporate law, governance, and immigration law in both Nigeria and the United Kingdom has spanned over two decades. His legal career includes prestigious roles such as Legal Manager at Consolidated Breweries Plc and Nigerian Breweries Plc, and later as Company Secretary/Legal Adviser at Benue Bottling Company Limited. He presently sits on the management of several blue chip companies within Nigeria and Europe

Currently, He is a founding partner at Victoria Chambers in Nigeria and Victoria Solicitors Limited in the UK, Chief Aiboni advises multinational corporations and has established a reputation for his meticulous attention to detail and results-oriented approach.

Sir David Ibhawoh

It gives me great pleasure to express my full support for High Chief Tosan Atle Aiboni's candidacy for President of the Lumen Christi Old Students Association (LUCOSA). Having known Tosan for over 35 years, I can attest to his unwavering professionalism and integrity. Our long-standing friendship has never compromised the lines of our professional relationship, a testament to his exceptional character. Tosan is not just a lawyer, friend, and confidant to me, but a pillar of reliability and astuteness. His innate ability to balance cordiality with astuteness makes him the ideal candidate to steer our alumni association towards greater heights. Under his leadership, I am confident that LUCOSA will undergo the necessary reformation it needs. Tosan's commitment to advancing the cause of our alumni by introducing forums for professional growth through networking is particularly exciting. This initiative not only aligns with my own values but has been a cornerstone of my success, and I believe it will greatly benefit our community. Moreover, Tosan's presidency could provide the perfect channel to connect talented alumni with potential opportunities within my organization, a possibility that has been challenging to realize in the past. I wholeheartedly endorse Tosan for President of LUCOSA and offer him my unwavering support. His vision for our association and his dedication to our collective progress assure me that we are on the brink of a new and dynamic chapter.
